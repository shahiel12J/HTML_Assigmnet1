Need For Speed


Release date:

March 14 2014

Plot:

A street racer who is framed for a crime and seeks to clear his name by competing in an underground race known as the De Leon. Need for Speed is an average action film that is likely to appeal to fans of the video game series, but may not resonate with a wider audience. It's a film that is mostly focused on the action and racing scenes rather than having a well-developed plot or characters.
