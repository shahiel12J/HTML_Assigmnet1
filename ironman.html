Iron Man


Release date:

1 May 2008

Plot:

Iron Man marked the beginning of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, and was followed by several sequels, as well as numerous other films in the MCU, making it an important and iconic film in the genre of superhero movies. The film tells the origin story of Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who is kidnapped by terrorists and forced to build a weapon. Instead, he builds a suit of powered armor to escape and decides to use the technology to fight against evil as the superhero Iron Man.
